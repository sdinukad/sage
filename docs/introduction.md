# Introduction to Sage

Sage is an AI-powered personal expense tracker designed to make financial management effortless and intelligent. By combining a natural chat interface with advanced local AI and powerful cloud processing, Sage helps you track, categorize, and understand your spending without the friction of traditional apps.

## Key Features

- **Chat-First Interaction**: Add expenses just by typing or talking to the app, like "Spent 1500 for grocery today".
- **100% Local Categorization**: Automatically categorizes your expenses using a fast, local AI model (BERT) for superior speed and privacy.
- **Real-time Summaries**: Get instant feedback on your monthly spending and budget status.
- **Privacy-First Design**: All intent and entity extraction happen locally on your device; no chat data is sent to external LLM providers.
- **Cross-Platform Access**: (Coming soon) Seamlessly sync your data between web and mobile interfaces.

## Why Sage?

Most expense trackers require manual entry through complex forms across multiple screens. Sage flips this model on its head by putting the conversation at the center. Whether you're recording a quick coffee or reviewing a month's worth of utilities, Sage understands you.

## Target Audience

- **Users**: Individuals looking for a simple, intuitive way to track their daily expenses without the hassle of manual bookkeeping.
- **Developers**: Engineers interested in building AI-integrated web and mobile applications using modern stacks like Next.js, Flutter, and Supabase.
