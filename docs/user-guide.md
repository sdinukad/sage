# User Guide

Welcome to Sage! This guide will help you get the most out of your AI-powered expense tracker.

## How to use Sage

Sage is designed to be as simple as sending a text message. You don't need to fill out complex forms or select categories manually.

### 1. Adding an Expense
Just type what you spent in the chat box. For example:
- "Spent 1500 for lunch"
- "Fuel for 5000"
- "Bought groceries for 3000 yesterday"

Sage will automatically:
1.  **Extract the amount** (e.g., 1500).
2.  **Determine the category** (e.g., Food).
3.  **Identify the date** (e.g., Today or Yesterday).

### 2. Viewing Your History
You can see all your recorded expenses in the **History** tab. You can filter them by date or category to see where your money is going.

### 3. Understanding the Dashboard
The **Dashboard** gives you a quick summary of your spending for the month.
- **Total Spend**: How much you've spent so far.
- **Budget Progress**: See if you're staying within your limits.
- **Top Categories**: Find out your biggest expenses at a glance.

---

## Tips for Best Results

- **Be Specific**: Use keywords like "lunch", "taxi", or "electricity" to help the AI categorize correctly.
- **Use Abbreviations**: You can use "k" for thousands (e.g., "Spent 2k for rent").
- **Check AI Suggestions**: Sometimes the AI might ask for clarification. Just respond to its questions in the chat!

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes! Sage uses local AI for initial processing right on your device. Your data is stored securely in your own Supabase account.

**Q: Can I edit an expense?**
A: Absolutely. You can edit any expense directly from the History tab.

**Q: Does it work offline?**
A: Sage can capture your intent while you're offline using its local AI. Once you're back online, it will sync your data to the cloud.
